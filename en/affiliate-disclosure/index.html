FTC Disclosure

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines, URCity discloses that some of the links on this website are affiliate links. This means that if you click on the link and make a purchase or sign up for a service, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you.

Our Commitment to Transparency

URCity is reader-supported. When you click on certain links on our website and make a purchase or sign up for a service, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This affiliate revenue helps us maintain and improve our free content.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that contain a tracking code. When you click these links, the merchant knows you came from URCity. If you make a purchase, we receive a small percentage of the sale.

Our Affiliate Partners

Editorial Independence

Our affiliate relationships do not influence our content. We:

  • Recommend services based on merit, not commission rates
  • Disclose affiliate relationships clearly

No Extra Cost to You

Affiliate commissions are paid by the merchant, not by you. The price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go directly to the merchant.

How to Support Us

If you find our content helpful, using our affiliate links is a great way to support our work at no cost to you.
