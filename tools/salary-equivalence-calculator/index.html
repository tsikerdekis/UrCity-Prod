How It Works

Enter your current salary and city, plus your destination city. We calculate the equivalent salary using cost of living indices from official sources.

Example

If you make $100,000 in New York City, you need approximately $62,000 in Austin to maintain the same standard of living. That’s because Austin’s cost of living is 36% lower.
