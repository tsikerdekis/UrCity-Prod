Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Publisher: Northshore Press (https://northshorepress.co)

Introduction

URCity (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device information

Via Forms:

  • Email address (if you subscribe to our newsletter)
  • Search queries (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

  • Improve website functionality and content
  • Analyze traffic patterns
  • Personalize content recommendations
  • Send newsletters (only if you opt in)
  • Comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our site

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — CDN and security

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with:

  • Analytics providers
  • Legal authorities (if required by law)

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Receive a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@urcity.com.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@urcity.com.
