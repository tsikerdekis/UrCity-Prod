Downtown Austin

Guide to Downtown Austin — cost of living, walkability, and things to do.

Downtown Austin Neighborhood Guide

Downtown Austin is the heart of the city — a compact, walkable district packed with high-rise condos, restaurants, bars, and live music venues.

Cost of Living

Downtown Austin is the most expensive neighborhood in the city. Expect to pay a premium for walkability and proximity to entertainment.

ExpenseCost
Median Rent (1BR)$2,500/month
Median Rent (2BR)$3,200/month
Median Condo Price$650,000
Price per Sq Ft$450

Walkability

  • Walk Score: 92 (Walker’s Paradise)
  • Transit Score: 78 (Excellent Transit)
  • Bike Score: 85 (Very Bikeable)

Things to Do

  • 6th Street — Live music bars and nightlife
  • Rainey Street — Bungalow bars and food trucks
  • Lady Bird Lake — Hiking and biking trails
  • Texas State Capitol — Free tours
  • Paramount Theatre — Historic performing arts venue

Who Lives Here?

Young professionals, tech workers, and empty nesters who want urban living without a car.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Walkable to everything
  • Best nightlife in Austin
  • No car needed
  • Close to major employers

Cons:

  • Most expensive area
  • Loud on weekends
  • Limited parking
  • Homeless population
