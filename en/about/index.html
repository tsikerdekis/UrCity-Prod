About URCity

Data-driven city comparison and relocation guides. We combine official government data with interactive tools to help you make informed decisions about where to live.

Our Mission

Moving to a new city is one of the biggest decisions you'll make. We believe everyone deserves access to clear, accurate, data-driven information to make that decision confidently.

Official Data Only

We use only verified, official sources: US Census Bureau, Bureau of Labor Statistics, Zillow Research, NOAA, FCC, and Tax Foundation. No guesswork, no crowdsourced estimates.

C2ER Methodology

Our cost of living indices follow the Council for Community and Economic Research (C2ER) standard. Custom scores combine multiple data points into easy-to-understand metrics.

Free & Independent

URCity is free to use. We may earn commissions through affiliate links at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep the data fresh and the site running without paywalls.

Data Sources

  • US Census Bureau — Demographics, income, housing
  • Bureau of Labor Statistics — Cost of living, wages
  • Zillow Research — Home values, rents
  • NOAA — Climate data
  • Federal Communications Commission — Internet availability
  • Tax Foundation — State and local tax rates

Important: All content on URCity is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial, legal, tax, or real estate advice. Data may not reflect current market conditions. Always consult qualified professionals before making relocation or financial decisions.
