How It Works

Our calculator uses official data from the Council for Community and Economic Research (C2ER) to compare costs across categories:

  • Housing — Rent and home prices
  • Groceries — Food and household items
  • Utilities — Electricity, gas, water, internet
  • Transportation — Gas, public transit, car insurance
  • Healthcare — Insurance and out-of-pocket costs
  • Miscellaneous — Entertainment, clothing, personal care
