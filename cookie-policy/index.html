Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Publisher: Northshore Press (https://northshorepress.co)

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies:

  • Required for the website to function properly
  • Cannot be disabled
  • These do not track you or store personal information

Analytics Cookies (with your consent):

  • We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our site
  • This helps us improve content and user experience
  • Google Analytics sets cookies only after you click “Accept” on our cookie banner
  • If you decline, no analytics cookies are set and no tracking occurs
  • Data collected is anonymized and processed by Google

Third-Party Cookies

We use one third-party service that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID: G-J1L2B8RMX6) — Used for aggregated, anonymized traffic analysis. See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

No other third parties set cookies on our site.

Managing Cookies

Via our cookie banner:

  • When you first visit, a banner appears at the bottom of the page
  • Click “Accept” to allow analytics cookies
  • Click “Decline” to refuse analytics cookies
  • Your choice is stored in your browser and remembered for future visits

Via your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Please note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our cookie practices change. Check this page for updates.
