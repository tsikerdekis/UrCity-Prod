About URCity

URCity helps people make informed decisions about where to live. We combine data from official government sources with interactive tools to give you the full picture of life in any US city.

Published by: Northshore Press (https://northshorepress.co)

Our Mission

Moving to a new city is one of the biggest decisions you’ll make. We believe everyone deserves access to clear, accurate, data-driven information to make that decision confidently.

Data Sources

We use only official, verified data sources:

  • US Census Bureau — Demographics, income, housing
  • Bureau of Labor Statistics — Cost of living, wages
  • Zillow Research — Home values, rents
  • National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration — Climate data
  • Federal Communications Commission — Internet availability
  • Tax Foundation — State and local tax rates

Methodology

Our cost of living indices are calculated using the Council for Community and Economic Research (C2ER) methodology. Our custom scores (Affordability, Remote Work, Livability) combine multiple data points into easy-to-understand metrics.

Important Disclaimers

All content on URCity is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial, legal, tax, or real estate advice. Data may not reflect current market conditions. Always consult qualified professionals before making relocation or financial decisions.
