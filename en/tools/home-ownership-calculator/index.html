What's Included

Our calculator accounts for:

  • Mortgage Payment — Principal + interest based on your down payment and rate
  • Property Taxes — Annual taxes divided monthly
  • Home Insurance — Estimated at 0.35% of home value annually
  • HOA Fees — Monthly association dues
  • Maintenance — Estimated at 1% of home value annually
  • Total 30-Year Cost — The shocking true number most buyers miss

Why This Matters

A $450,000 home doesn’t cost $450,000. Over 30 years, you’ll pay closer to $900,000 when you factor in interest, taxes, and insurance. Know the real number before you buy.
